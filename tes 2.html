String instruments are musical instruments that produce sound through the vibration of strings.
These instruments have one or more strings stretched tightly between two points, which can be plucked, bowed, or struck to create musical tones.
String instruments can be further classified into several categories based on their design and playing techniques.

My Drunk Art

Keyboard instruments are musical instruments that produce sound by pressing keys on a keyboard.
The keys are typically arranged in a linear fashion, and when a key is pressed, it activates a mechanism that produces sound, whether through strings, pipes, or electronic means.
Keyboard instruments are diverse and have been an integral part of music for centuries.

My Sadness Art

Percussion instruments are a diverse group of musical instruments that produce sound through the striking, shaking, or scraping of their surfaces or materials.
They are an essential component of various musical traditions and can be found in a wide range of musical styles and cultural contexts.

My Lonelyness Art

Woodwind instruments are a family of musical instruments that produce sound when air is blown across or through a wooden reed or a similar material.
While the term "woodwind" traditionally referred to instruments made of wood,
many modern woodwind instruments are constructed from various materials, including metals and plastics.
Woodwind instruments are known for their ability to produce a wide range of tones and dynamics.
